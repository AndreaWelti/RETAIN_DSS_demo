📥 Tell us about your tarpaulin

What is it made of? How dirty is it? How old is it?

Imagine you found an old truck blanket made of two plastics — PVC (the soft, flexible part) and PET (the strong fibres inside, like in a plastic bottle). 🎒

Before we recycle it, we need to tell the computer a few things about it. It's like filling in a school report card — but for the tarpaulin!
PVC is the same plastic used in garden hoses and raincoats. PET is the same plastic used in fizzy-drink bottles!

🎮 Try it! Move the sliders — hover the ? buttons for details.

Material profile — the computer sees this "shape" for every tarpaulin

🧠 The AI brain predicts what will happen

No real experiment needed — the computer already knows!

Imagine a very clever student who has watched 10 000 recycling experiments and took perfect notes every time. Now, whenever you give them a new tarpaulin, they can guess the result without running a single experiment! 🔮

RETAIN DSS uses 21 such AI models (called XGBoost). Each model learned a different thing: one predicts purity, another predicts energy use, another predicts how much material you get back, and so on.
XGBoost won dozens of data science competitions around the world — it is like the world-champion student!

Each model gets 14 numbers as input and produces one prediction. Route 1 → 5 models, Route 2 → 7 models, Route 3 → 9 models = 21 models total.

3

♻️ Three different recycling recipes

Like three different ways to cook the same ingredient

There is no single "perfect" way to recycle an old tarpaulin. RETAIN tests three completely different methods at the same time — and then picks the best one for YOUR tarpaulin. Click each card to learn more!
🔨

T3.1 — Mechanical Recycling

Grind the tarpaulin into tiny pieces, then use electricity to sort the PVC from the PET — like a really smart sieve! No chemicals needed.

🔌 Low energy · No chemicals
🧪

T3.2 — Solvent Recycling (Texyloop)

Dip the pieces in a hot magic liquid (solvent). The PVC melts into it, PET stays solid. Then cool the liquid — PVC re-forms as super-clean granules!

🌟 Highest PVC purity
💰

T3.3 — Additive Extraction

Wash out the expensive chemical additives (plasticisers & stabilisers) without dissolving the plastic. Like extracting the special sauce!

💎 Recovers high-value chemicals

4

🧬 Finding the best recipe settings

Trying millions of combinations — like the world's biggest cooking show!

For each recycling recipe, there are 6 "dials" to turn (speed, temperature, time…). If each dial has 100 possible positions, that's 100⁶ = one trillion combinations! We can't try them all.

Instead, RETAIN uses a genetic algorithm (NSGA-II) — an idea copied from nature. 🐛→🦋

  1. Start with 100 random guesses (generation 1)
  2. Keep the best ones, mix them together, make tiny mutations
  3. Repeat 200 times
  4. After 200 generations, the survivors are near-perfect!
Nature invented genetic algorithms! Animals that are better at surviving have more children — after millions of generations, amazing creatures exist. We do the same, but in 200 steps! 🦁
NSGA-II key settings:
  • Population: 100 solutions per generation
  • Generations: 200 rounds of evolution
  • Crossover (SBX): η = 15
  • Mutation (PM): η = 20
  • Objectives: 5 goals simultaneously

5

📐 Comparing the three winners

Finding the route that covers the most "treasure space"

After the algorithm finishes, each route gives us not just one answer, but a whole set of great answers called a Pareto front. Think of it as a menu of the best trade-offs:

"If you want very clean PVC, you need more energy. If you want less energy, the PVC will be a bit less clean."

To pick the best route, we measure how much "treasure space" each front covers — the Hypervolume Indicator (HV). Bigger area = better route! 🎁
The hypervolume is computed in 5 dimensions (5 objectives) in the real tool — but the idea is exactly the same as the 2D picture on the right!

6

🏆 The recommendation — your recycling champion!

The tool tells you exactly what to do

After comparing the three routes, RETAIN DSS names a winner — the route that gives the best overall balance of purity, yield, energy, quality, and profit for YOUR tarpaulin.

It also gives you the exact settings to use: how fast to run the machine, what temperature to set, how long to wait… Every dial, already optimised for you! 🎛️
Finding these settings manually would take years of experiments. RETAIN DSS finds them in about 60 seconds!
